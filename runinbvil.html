Group Runs

  • Tuesdays, 6pm, Hills at the Shrine
  • Thursdays, 6pm, downtown Belleville
  • Saturdays, 8am, downtown Belleville

Tuesdays -Hills at the Shrine

Currently, we meet at the Shrine of Our Lady of the Snows on Tuesday nights at 6:00pm for hill workouts. We meet in the parking lot by the restaurant. Please make certain that you are on our Yahoo Group and/or Facebook Group so that you receive information on any changes to the time or location. This will continue until daylight savings time ends in the fall. In the fall (after Daylight Savings Time ends), we will meet at Southwestern Illinois College (SWIC) on Tuesday evenings at 6:00pm for a run along a well lit trail. We meet in the parking lot just north of the Metrolink stop. The trail is about 1.4 miles around, it’s well lit, there are good bathrooms, and there is water. Please make certain that you are on our Facebook Group and Yahoo Group so that you receive information on any changes to the time or location. This will continue until daylight savings time begins in the spring. Toolen’s Running Start also hosts a Tuesday night speedwork at 5:30pm from their store in Shiloh. Call the store at (618) 628-9898 for more information.

Thursdays -Social Night

We meet on Thursday evenings at 6:00 pm in the parking lot at the northwest corner of “A” and “North Jackson” streets, adjacent to Casa Maria Mexican Grill (100 N. High St). We run a course that consists of a 3 mile loop as shown below, although many people run the 5 mile loop shown below with the Saturday run. Please see the temporary route during construction on the trail – 3 Mile Route – www.plotaroute.com/route/393204 - This is referred to as social night since many of our members often go out for dinner or a drink afterwards.

Saturdays -The Long Run

We meet on Saturday mornings at 8:00 am in the parking lot behind Margaritas Mexican Restaurant in downtown Belleville. We run a course that consists of a 5 mile loop. However, this distance and start times can vary during the marathon training sessions. Please see the temporary route during construction on the trail – 5 Mile Route – www.plotaroute.com/route/393205

BAM Awards


The goal of the BRC Awesome Member (BAM) Award is to encourage all members to

Attend group runs

Volunteer at BRC events

Attend BRC meetings (yes, we have to bribe you to attend our meetings)

Show your BRC colors at other events, namely races

BRC Awesome Member Award points and awards are based on participation in, not results of, these activities. Every January, during our Annual Awards Banquet, the top 10 BAM point-getters will be recognized for their accomplishments and BAM prizes will be awarded.



Most Awesome Rewards

1st place – $125 Gift Card to Toolen’s Running Start

2nd place – $50 Race Reimbursement

3rd place – $25 Race Reimbursement

*The next 7 point-getters will receive a BRC Shirt. Also not too shabby.


Details/Rules of Competition

You must be a registered member of the BRC. If you are not an “official” member, you can register at https://secure.getmeregistered.com/get_information.php?event_id=126263

Members must race/volunteer in visible BRC gear. It can be a T-shirt, singlet, hoodie, etc. Visit our online store to purchase one!

Members earn 1 point for every full mile raced in any official race. For example, a 5K (3.1 miles) will count as 3 points.

Members earn the same number of points if they VOLUNTEER at one of these events. We understand that you may be required to wear a volunteer shirt if you are volunteering at a race. If so, try to wear a BRC hat/visor or something. Or just wear your BRC shirt under the volunteer shirt.

Members earn 15 points for volunteering at either of the annual Trashy Runs.

Members earn 5 points for attending a BRC meeting.

Members earn 2 points/hour for volunteering at the Chili Cookoff.

Members earn 1 extra point for posting a photo of yourself (in a BRC shirt) accomplishing one of the tasks above on our facebook group.

Members earn 1 point per group run. To earn these points, the member must be tagged in the picture that’s posted to the BRC Public Group with the #BRCGroupRun17 hash tag.

Members must submit their race results within 2 WEEKS of completing an event. Results must be submitted by completing the form below. Any running or walking race of any distance will be eligible. Only the running portion of a multisport event will count toward point totals. Unofficial fun runs are excluded.

Permanent tattoo of the BRC logo earns you a lifetime BAM Award (and probably a lifetime of regrets).

Points will generally be accumulated only during a calendar year (January 1 through December 31).
