Local Resources

Here are a few local resources. If you want information on local running clubs, check out Run Belleville

Belleville Parks & Recreation (Belleville Parks and Recreation takes care of the bike trail and parks that we run through.)

Get Up & GO (Get Up Go is a Health and Fitness Challenge for Communities, Groups and Organizations of St. Clair County, Illinois.)

MEPRD(Metro East Park and Recreation District shall have as its primary duty the development, operation, and maintenance of a public system of interconnecting trails and parks throughout St Clair and Madison County.)

Trailnet (Trailnet is a St. Louis based non-profit organization with a 20-year history of Promoting Active Living as a way of life that encourages people to integrate physical activity into their daily routines.)

Big Hump( A hash is a run with beer at the beginning, middle, and end, followed by a lot more beer and some very silly songs.)

Bell-Scott Hash Harriers (Southern Illinois Hashers. They tend to meet somewhere in the area between Troy, Cahokia, Columbia and Prairie du Rocher. Quite a few are in Belleville. They currently run on 1st, 3rd and 5th Saturdays.)


Running Resources

Cool Running (Coolrunning.com is a running website. The link takes you to their aches and pains section to help you out with any running pains that you might be having.)

Running In The USA(Runningintheusa.com is a website with national listings of races, running clubs, etc.)

US Race Calendar (The US Race Calendar is for promoting running and racing. All race directors, event managers and running clubs can use the US Race Calendar for their race website.)

Athlinks (Athlinks is the largest results database for competitive endurance athletes in the world. They pull in times for running races, triathlon, swimming, cycling, mountain biking … pretty much any timed endurance sport that you can think of – if they can find the results, they will add them to the database and link them to members’ accounts.)

Map My Run (Mapmyrun.com is a social wellness-oriented website that allows mapping routes, tracking fitness, etc.)

Sports Injuries Guide (A web resource for sports related injuries)
