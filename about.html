About BRC

The Belleville Running Club, informally organized in 2005, was founded with the simple goal of trying to assemble like minded individuals (runners) to share the often mundane task of the “long run.” Over the years, the BRC has developed into quite a bit more. Although we have only recently become officially organized, many of us have formed some pretty good friendships while developing a civic-minded group that promotes and supports running in the Belleville area. The official purpose of the BRC (per our bylaws) is to promote and encourage running as a sport and as a means of healthful exercise in our community; to promote and conduct races or other activities; to disseminate information, and to offer opportunities for organized and informal socializing by members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Hopefully this will answer most of your questions about the Belleville Running Club. If you have any additional questions, please feel free to e-mail us. If you are a beginner runner and looking for the FAQ’s on the beginner’s program, please go to the beginner’s page.

Are there any water stops along your runs?

There are water fountains and restrooms at the Belleville MetroLink station and we always break for water at the end of each lap.

What if I’m a really slow / real fast runner?

Our ultimate goal is to have a diverse group or runners who run a wide variety of paces.

Can I buy a BRC Shirt?

Please see our Members page for ordering information or visit or online store.

What if I don’t want to run a billion miles like some of you?

We typically run a 3-mile or 5-mile loop. Feel free to run as many or as few laps as you’d like.

Do I have to be a member to join the group runs?

No. We do encourage you to join if you find that you enjoy the club because you’ll get benefits such as discounted BRC merchandise, discounts on member friendly races, discount codes from our sponsors, and more!

How much does membership cost?

Please see our Members page for current prices and details on member benefits.
